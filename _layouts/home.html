Bio



I am a Ph.D. student in the computer science department of Northwestern University. I am broadly interested in the database. In detail, I am passionate about security and privacy-preserving database systems by applying cryptographic techniques. During my Ph.D., I will research under the guidance of Prof. Jennie Rogers.

My goal is to improve the database so that more people can efficiently analyze data and trust our systems.
