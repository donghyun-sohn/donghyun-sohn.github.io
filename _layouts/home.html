Bio



Hello, my name is Donghyun Sohn and I am currently a Ph.D. student in the computer science department at Northwestern University. My research focus is on database systems, with a particular interest in security and privacy-preserving techniques using cryptography. I am fortunate to have the opportunity to work with Prof. Jennie Rogers as my advisor during my Ph.D. studies.

My goal as a researcher is to make advancements in the field of databases that allow more people to confidently and efficiently analyze data, while also ensuring the trustworthiness of our systems. I am excited to continue exploring this area of study and making a positive impact on the field.
